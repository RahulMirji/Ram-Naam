# Privacy Policy for Ram Naam Tracker

**Effective Date: March 15, 2026**

Rahul Mirji ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the Ram Naam Tracker mobile application.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

**Ram Naam Tracker is a privacy-first application.**

- **Personal Data**: We do NOT collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.
- **Local Storage ONLY**: All data you enter into the app (Jaap counts, targets, etc.) is stored **locally on your device** using an internal SQLite database. We do not have access to this data, and it is never transmitted to our servers.
- **Backups**: If you use the backup/restore feature, the backup file is created on your local storage. You choose where to save or share it. We do not have access to your backup files.

## 2. Permissions

The application may request the following permissions:
- **Storage/Media**: To allow you to export and import backup files of your Jaap data.
- **Internet**: Only used if you choose to share your progress or look at external resources (like documentation). No tracking data is sent.

## 3. Third-Party Services

The app does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs that collect your data. 

## 4. Children's Privacy

The application does not collect any information from children. Since all data remains on the device, it is safe for users of all ages.

## 5. Security

We use standard security practices to ensure the integrity of the application. However, since the data is stored on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security.

## 6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
